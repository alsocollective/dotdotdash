What is Studios on Sustainability?

Knowing that there are a lot of really important grassroots organizations out there that need a bit of help, we founded of Studios On Sustainability. It is a program that brings together the volunteered services of creative individuals with grassroots organizations. We bridge existing communications challenges to create valuable solutions that help solve environmental and social issues. We turn audiences into communities.
